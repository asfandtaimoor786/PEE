Summary

Founded in 1985, PPE made engineering innovations the prime directive. Since the beginning our engineers improved the horsepower and torque of the duramax diesel platform with the PPE Duramaximizer.

PPE continued to innovate creating products to enhance and improve the Duramax diesel platform, systematically finding and improving “weak links” which would fail under the new high horsepower and torque demands.

Our engineers built on their success by turning their sights on Ford and Dodge diesel platforms. With each improvement, they learned how to make clean, efficient power, while improv ing fuel mileage and the drivers experience.

Today, PPE has expanded and is now focusing more than 30 years of experience to create a new line of intelligent, heavy-duty upgrades for GM, Ford, Dodge/Ram, Jeep andmany others.
